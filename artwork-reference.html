Artwork reference: what the level should feel like

Use the supplied artwork as the visual north star. The level should read as a playable bowling lane first, then as a crowded arcade-horror scene with undead staff, cracked living pins, green ectoplasm, orange neon, moonlit blue shadows, and a dangerous back-machine focal point.

Local assetsArt bible
Cold exterior moonUse blue light through broken windows so the alley feels connected to the graveyard outside.
Neon signage layerOrange and green signs create readable silhouettes without turning the whole scene one color.
Clear lane readKeep a central ball path visible even when the sides are busy with props, fog, and enemies.
Hero pin threatThe living pins are the identity of the game. Backlight them, give them eye glow, and keep their faces readable.
Visual priority orderFrom gameplay to dressing
  1. 1. Ball path: approach, foul line, lane boards, arrows, gutters, and pin rack must read in one glance.
  2. 2. Pin deck: zombie pins, back machine, green fog, and red danger light form the horror target.
  3. 3. Side story: arcade cabinets, scoreboards, staff zombies, posters, shoes, and broken signs sit outside the clean play path.
  4. 4. Ceiling depth: hanging cables, missing panels, flickering tubes, and fan shadows create height without blocking aim.
  5. 5. FX polish: sparks, slime, dust, steam, and debris should support impact moments, not hide the ball.
Palette splitUse as lighting targets
Moon/shadow range blue-black shadows, window shafts, exterior graveyard silhouettes.
Ectoplasm range sickly green eyes, fog, ball energy, slime streaks, and pin glow.
Neon/danger range orange signs, red warning bulbs, sparks, and hot back-machine accents.
Material range wet wood, stained concrete, rusty steel, cracked ceramic, dirty white pin paint.

Key art: lane chase

Use for hero composition, ball impact energy, lane reflections, logo treatment, and foreground threat scale.

Impact art: strike burst

Use for VFX timing, debris arcs, green energy, crowd pressure, and pin collapse silhouettes.

Zombie pin model target

Cracked ceramic, red band, exposed skull face, glowing eyes, slime, chipped base, and asymmetrical damage.
