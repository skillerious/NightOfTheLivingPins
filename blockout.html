Step-by-step cube placement

  1. Open Window > Place Actors.
  2. Choose Shapes and drag Cube into the viewport.
  3. With the cube selected, rename it in the Outliner or at the top of Details.
  4. In Details, type the Location and Scale from the table.
  5. Drag the actor into 00_BLOCKOUT or the more specific Outliner folder.
  6. Press Ctrl + W to duplicate similar pieces, then rename and update the numbers.

Viewport checks while blocking out

  • Use Top orthographic view to check lane width, gutters, rails, and pin deck alignment.
  • Use Right or Front orthographic view to check ceiling height and back-wall scale.
  • Use Lit for rough readability and Wireframe when shapes overlap.
  • Press F on a selected actor when you get lost in the scene.
